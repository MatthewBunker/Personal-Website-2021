Welcome to my website

Hi, my name is Matthew Bunker and I am a Junior at Texas A&M University, getting a bachelors in computer science and a minor in business. Throughout my time here at Texas A&M, I have been involved in many student organizations such F.L.A.S.H a service organization for freshman where I learned many valuable lessons such as the importance of having a community at college, and helping the less fortunate. I have also, been apart of the dozen ultimate frisbee club team where I have learned the value of teamwork, and pursuing a healthy lifestyle during college (especially during the Covid semesters). Next, I have been involved in many student worker positions such as peer teaching for an introductory engineering course where I guide students in introductory coding skills, this has taught me the lessons of leadership, and time management by balancing school and work life. Lastly, I have recently started a web development position for the Carter-Sowell psychology research lab, this has given me the ability to contribute to benefiting society while pursuing something that I'm passionate about. Besides my student organizations, I have been excited to have the opportunity to pursue many classes that have given me many exciting projects to work on as a team where I have been able to gain critical skills that will help me in the industry. Other than school-related activities I like to hang out with friends, play video games, go boating and play, watch, analyze, and read all about sports. Overall my time in college has gifted me with many exciting opportunities that have made me a more well-rounded individual and have made me excited to use these new-found skills and hobbies in the future.

Goals and Ambitions

It is an interesting part of my life right now, almost graduating college and for the first time ever I have no vision for the future. The road to school is almost over. Is it scary? Yes, but the road for learning and the time left for ambition has just started. There are so many things I want to accomplish during this short time I have on earth. I have always thought of making a difference in this world and not be another person that comes and goes and I am determined to do that. I have always been taught that I should strive to be more successful than my parents because they have laid a solid framework for me to work with and it's my duty to raise that framework for my children. With that said, my personal goals would be to travel to as many countries in the world as possible, I would like to go to every baseball stadium in the country, and I would love to raise a family to be able to spend my life with. As far as my career goals go, I would like to work for my dream company ESPN or for a sports organization like the Houston Astros, Houston Rockets, or Dallas Cowboys. Overall my definition will not be how much money I have, or what type of house or cars I own, but will be that I don't regret doing or not doing something at the end of my life.
